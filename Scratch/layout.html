is a list of the cities I served in, a list of my companions, as well as a brief description as to why my mission is so important to me.

Description

As a missionary, I served in 4 cities with 13 different companions. In each area and with each companion, I learned a lot and each of them has contributed to the person I am today. Although my mission got off to a rocky start, I loved being able meet new people, try new foods, love the Lords Children, and do what Jesus Christ would do if he were here.
